What is BYLD?

We are the software development club of IIITD. We bring together passionate individuals who love to collaborate, innovate, create and build.

What we do at BYLD?

  • Hackathons
  • Darwin Devs
  • Tech Talks
  • Participate in Open Source Events
  • Industry collaboration
  • Be part of a community

Some member only perks

  • Exclusive membership to alum group
  • Member only hack sessions
  • Opportunity to build projects for institute
  • Access to dedicated computing resources

How to become a member?

  • Win a renowned hackathon
  • GSOC, Outreachy
  • Through active participation (NEW)
  • Organize sessions with Byld (NEW)
